How it works

Three steps. Zero fluff.

No manual research. No reading through pages of news. Sentwell does the heavy lifting so you can focus on decisions.

01
📥
Enter tickers
Type up to 3 stock symbols — AAPL, TSLA, NVDA — and hit Analyse. That's it.
02
🧠
FinBERT analyses
Our NLP engine reads up to 25 live headlines per ticker and scores each one as positive, negative, or neutral with a confidence score.
03
⚡
AI summarises
A GPT-powered summary distils the sentiment picture into 3 actionable sentences — the overall mood, near-term outlook, and key risks.
04
📊
Compare & export
Compare multiple tickers side by side, track sentiment trends over time, and download the full dataset as CSV.
Features

Everything you need to
trade with context.

Built for speed, depth, and clarity. No noise — just signal.

NLP · FinBERT
Financial-grade sentiment
Powered by ProsusAI's FinBERT — a BERT model fine-tuned on thousands of financial news articles. Far more accurate than general-purpose sentiment models on market text.
Real-time
Live news, live scores
Pulls from Yahoo Finance's live news feed. Every analysis reflects what's happening right now — not yesterday's data.
AI summaries · LLM
Instant market intelligence
An LLM synthesises the sentiment data into a concise 3-sentence brief: overall mood, near-term outlook, and risk/opportunity signals.
Multi-ticker
Analyse up to 3 tickers
Compare AAPL vs MSFT vs NVDA simultaneously. Side-by-side charts, confidence breakdowns, and a verdict table in one view.
Trend analysis
Sentiment over time
Track how the market mood for a ticker has shifted day by day. Spot when coverage turned negative before the price moved.
Watchlist · Export
Save & download
Build a persistent watchlist of your key tickers. Export any analysis as CSV for your own modelling or backtesting workflows.

  • Fundamental valuation and earnings trajectory uncertainty

  • Short-term negative news contrasting with longer-term positive structural thesis

About the builder

Built by S.A.M. Rizvi.

I'm a data scientist and ML engineer with a focus on NLP and financial applications. Sentwell started as a weekend project to scratch my own itch — I wanted to see market sentiment at a glance without reading through 30 news tabs before the opening bell.

The older version lives on GitHub. This version — with FinBERT, AI summaries, multi-ticker comparison, and trend analysis — is deployed on Hugging Face Spaces and actively maintained.
